Let's Learn About Dolphins!

There are 36 different species of marine dolphins. They live in almost all aquatic environments including oceans, coasstal, estuarine, and freshwater.

Dolphins' Favorite Foods

  • Fish
  • Shrimp
  • Squid
  • Jellyfish

    • Dolphins can live up to 50 years!

    Dolphins are part color blind! They can see in shades of grey, as well as the blue- green spectrum!
